Form of Anxiety (2020)

 

Graphics, Printings

 

I assumed a form of Anxiety that I have deep inside my heart. Sometimes it's really heavy that I hardly treat them, also it becomes light.

I intended to make an image looks not only heavy, but also seemed to fly away at any moment. I printed this form on A1 size paper.
