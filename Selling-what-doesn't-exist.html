Selling what doesn't exist

2025 · Poster, Self-Initiated, Exhibition

I participated in the poster exhibition held at Suwon Nammun Market with 220 years of history. 100 posters of designers, artists, students, and professors were displayed at the outdoor exhibition hall and Yeongdong Market.

In line with the exhibition theme of "Selling What Doesn't Exist" I designed "What We Can't Buy."
