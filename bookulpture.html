BOOKULPTURE

2023 · Site Specific Graphic Design, Book Design, Editorial Design, Sculpture

The boundary between graphic design and sculpture has become blurred. Graphic design, once confined to flat surfaces, has rapidly expanded not only to websites and digital interfaces but also to three-dimensional forms often referred to as sculptures.

This raises the question: where does graphic design end, and where does sculpture begin? BOOKULPTURE explores this elusive boundary by combining the two into a single form – a book and sculpture simultaneously.

그래픽 디자인과 조각의 경계는 점점 흐려지고 있습니다. 평면에 머물렀던 그래픽 디자인은 웹과 디지털 인터페이스를 넘어, 때로는 조각이라고 불리는 입체적 형태로까지 확장되고 있습니다.

그렇다면 그래픽 디자인은 어디까지이고, 조각은 어디서부터 시작될까요? BOOKULPTURE는 책과 조각을 하나의 형태로 결합함으로써, 이 모호한 경계를 탐구하고자 합니다.
