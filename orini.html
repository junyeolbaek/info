Orini

2019 · Visual identity, branding

Orini is a toy brand for children inspired by nature. The core idea is “Nature, naturally to i (kids)”—bringing natural textures, colors, and forms closer to everyday play.

This branding project received a Bronze award in the “Branding for Social Change” category at the Indigo Design Award 2020 (Amsterdam-based).
