Raw Archive

2023 · Scenography, Spatial Design

Spatial Design for Raw Archive, the graduate exhibition of Visual Communication Design at Hongik University. The exhibition treats “Archive” not as a fixed collection of finished works, but as an ongoing process of accumulating, rearranging, and re-reading materials. The scenography organizes books, prints, and objects into a spatial system that remains open, modular, and constantly reconfigured by viewers.
