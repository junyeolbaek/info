CC2

2025 · Book Design

CC is a publishing project by AABB that documents the activities of contemporary visual artists onto the medium of a book. This issue, CC2, introduces the work of Hyunjin Kim, Kiljong Park, Gunjung Lee, Particlefield, Kiwoong Choi, formless twins.
