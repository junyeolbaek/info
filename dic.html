Dictionary of Uncatchable Mass (2019)

 

Editorial design

 

Normally, the mass, lump is tangible and can be clumped or touched.

However, we human sometimes use word `lump` to ungraspable things. Like not in a real world things.

For example, digital datas can be a lump, our emotion can be a lump.

In this dictionary, you can see introduction and exploration of Uncatchable mass in our world.
